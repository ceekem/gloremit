Our Services

About Us

GlobalPay is about delivering instant money transfer service to beneficiaries 24/7 around the World. We understand how technology is moving things so fast. With this in mind we have combined experience and skills to built all in one system that offers several services like; Mobile wallet transfer, Instant bank account credit, Airtime top up, Bill payment for our Money Transfer Clients and Payment gateway service for Merchants. Our flagship prepaid card service is in line government’s cashless economy programme. In everything we offer seek to bring convenience to our customers at the cheapest cost. All our MTO clients integrates into our system to deliver uninterrupted services to their customers.

Vision

We intend to set the standards of money remittances by making use of the contemporary technology available to transfer digits across the globe within the fastest possible time at the lowest possible cost.

Mission

Our mission is to offer a truly fair service to all customers and respect them as they send their hard earned money to their loved ones whose lives may depend on these remittances. And also continually give our clients convenience, cheap fees, reliable and secure service.
